USB Cabling

USB cabling for the 1ch Gateway is really simple:

  • Make sure your PC acceps USB devices and recorgnizes them
  • Make sure the you have a good cable that not only connects the power-supply but can also be used by the Serial Port.
  • Although long cables seem to work good, as well as cheap cables from Aliexpress, please use a well known Micro USB cable of one of your mobile phones first so you are sure the cable works OK.

If all goes well, and you have more than one gateway connected you can use color coding to distinguish between cables and gateways etc.
